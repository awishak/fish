About

Aquariums Built with Care

At Ishak Custom Aquariums, every build starts with a conversation. We work closely with each client to design an aquarium that fits their space, their taste, and the species they want to keep — whether that's a lush planted freshwater tank, a vibrant reef, or a dramatic custom installation.

Based in the Bay Area, we handle everything from design and construction to stocking and initial setup. Our specialty is freshwater, but we build across the full spectrum.
